Pancakes

These pancakes are the ultimate breakfast comfort food—soft, fluffy, and golden-brown with a tender crumb that melts in your mouth. Made with a simple batter of flour, sugar, milk, egg, and butter, they come together quickly and cook up beautifully in a hot skillet. Whether stacked high with butter and maple syrup or topped with fruits, whipped cream, or nuts, they're endlessly adaptable and always delicious. Their light and airy texture combined with a slightly crisp exterior make them a staple for lazy weekends and brunch gatherings alike.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Tags: Breakfast, Sweet, Easy

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a skillet and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake and cook until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden. Serve with syrup.
