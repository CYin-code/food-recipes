Chicken Caesar Salad

This Chicken Caesar Salad is a refreshing yet hearty dish that combines the crisp crunch of romaine lettuce with the juicy tenderness of grilled chicken breast. Golden-brown croutons add texture, while shavings of Parmesan cheese provide a salty, nutty kick. The creamy Caesar dressing, with its bold garlic and anchovy undertones, ties all the elements together into a balanced and satisfying salad. Perfect as a light lunch or a healthy dinner, this classic salad delivers bold flavors in every bite while remaining incredibly simple to prepare and enjoy.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 2
  • Tags: Salad, Chicken, Healthy
