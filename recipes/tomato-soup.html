Tomato Soup

This Simple Tomato Soup is the essence of comfort in a bowl, featuring the rich, tangy flavor of ripe tomatoes blended into a smooth, creamy consistency. With just a few ingredients like onion, garlic, canned tomatoes, and vegetable broth, it comes together effortlessly and simmers to develop deep, layered flavors. A swirl of cream adds a touch of indulgence, while fresh basil or cracked pepper on top elevates the presentation. It's perfect for chilly evenings or a quick lunch, especially when paired with a crusty grilled cheese sandwich or warm bread on the side.

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 2
  • Tags: Soup, Comfort Food, Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • ½ cup cream (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan and sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Blend the soup until smooth (optional).
  4. Stir in cream, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve warm, garnished with basil.
